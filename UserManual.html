Aikido Tracker

Welcome to the Aikido Tracker user's manual. This application is designed to help Aikido practitioners keep track of their sessions, maintain a consistent practice history, and export their progress for long-term recording.

Simple & Efficient

Designed with a dark, aesthetic UI that matches the focus and discipline of Aikido training. Record your attendance with just a single tap.

Getting Started

When you first open the app, you will be greeted by the Dashboard. To begin tracking your sessions, follow these steps:

  1. Enter your Full Name in the input field.
  2. Tap the "Mark Attendance" button.
  3. Once your name is set, the input field will hide to keep the UI clean, and your name will appear in the session counter.

Core Features

Session Tracking

Every time you practice, tap "Mark Attendance". The app automatically records the current date and increments your total session count.

Practice History

All your sessions are listed in the history section. Each entry shows the date of the session in a clear, card-based layout.

Edit Records

Made a mistake or forgot to record a session on a specific date? You can tap the Edit button on any history item to modify the recorded date.

  • History List: Scrollable list of all your training milestones.

Data Management

Exporting Records

Tap the "Export Record" button to save your entire history as a file. The app generates a .txt file containing a JSON formatted list of all your sessions.

File Location: Downloads/Aikido_Record_[Name]_[Timestamp].txt

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using a secure SQLite database. No data is sent to external servers, ensuring your privacy.
